40% of Road Hazards Are Due to Damaged Road Furniture

Our AI-powered system helps detect, monitor, and assess the condition of essential road furniture like Traffic Signs, Guardrails & Barriers, Streetlights, Pedestrian Crosswalk Signals, Reflectors, etc. in real-time, ensuring safer roads and reducing long-term maintenance costs. Benefits include:

  • Early detection of damaged or missing road furniture.
  • AI-driven monitoring for inspections 10x faster and 30% more accurate than traditional manual methods.
  • Proactive maintenance to extend the life of road furniture, cutting repair and replacement costs by up to 50%. 
  • Improved traffic safety and flow, with optimized visibility and functionality of road signs, lights, and barriers.
  • Data-driven insights for infrastructure managers, developers, and policymakers to prioritize high-risk areas and allocate resources efficiently.
